Local makers

Art, galleries and handmade finds

Golden Bay has a strong independent creative streak. This page gives visitors an on-site starting point for galleries, sculpture, and local makers around Pohara and Takaka before they decide where to click next.

What to expect

Golden Bay has real local character

This is a good area for wandering through galleries, artist studios, sculpture gardens, and small spaces with work you are unlikely to see anywhere else. Takaka gives you the greatest concentration, while the road between Takaka and Pohara has a few memorable stops of its own.

Some spaces are seasonal or open by chance, so a quick check before heading out is still worthwhile.

Quick tips
  • Takaka is the best base for a casual gallery wander.
  • The road to Pohara includes sculpture and creative studio stops worth watching for.
  • If you are after a broader local arts list, the Golden Bay Arts directory is the best backup.
