About the Region

Coastal charm at the edge of Abel Tasman

Pohara is one of the best places to stay in Golden Bay if you want easy beach access, relaxed seaside accommodation, and quick day trips to Abel Tasman National Park. The village is known for its long sweep of golden sand, calm swimming water, and easy access to walks, cafes, galleries, and local food.

From a Pohara base, you can explore the Abel Tasman Coast Track, visit Te Waikoropupu Springs, swim at Ligar Bay and Tata Beach, and discover some of the best things to do in Golden Bay without spending your whole trip in the car. It is a practical, family-friendly base with real local character.

Start with the best beaches around Pohara, add an Abel Tasman day walk from the Golden Bay side, then round out the trip with wildlife stops, places to eat in Pohara and Takaka, and a few local gallery and craft stops.
