Photo language

Let the images feel expensive.

The current site can be pushed much further by trusting the imagery and reducing filler. This concept uses oversized photography, quieter overlays, and stronger spacing so the place feels more desirable.

It also gives us a cleaner path to replace the remaining placeholder content and build out accommodation, local area, and booking moments in a more cohesive way.
