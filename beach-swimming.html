Beach & Swimming

Pohara’s golden sand beach is sheltered, safe, and perfect for families. Enjoy swimming, paddleboarding, and kayaking in calm waters all year round.

Overview

Why Pohara Beach?

Pohara Beach offers a long stretch of golden sand, gentle slopes, and protected waters thanks to the surrounding headlands. It’s ideal for a relaxed day by the sea, with plenty of space for picnics, beach games, and sunset strolls.

Swimming

The beach is sheltered and has a gradual entry, making it safe for swimmers of all abilities. Lifeguards are often present during summer weekends. Water quality is generally excellent; check local notices for any temporary warnings.

Paddleboarding

Calm morning conditions are perfect for stand-up paddleboarding. You can hire boards in Pohara or bring your own. The sheltered bay means you won’t face unexpected surf or currents.

Kayaking

Explore the coastline from the water. Paddle toward the northern headland for scenic views, or head south toward gentle estuaries. Rental options are available locally.

Family Friendly

Safe for All Ages

Pohara Beach is known for being family-friendly. The water is shallow near the shore, and there are no strong rips in the main swimming area. Still, always supervise children and follow basic water safety.

Tips

Make the Most of Your Visit

  • Bring water shoes if you're sensitive to shells/pebbles in the shallows.
  • Arrive early in peak season (Dec–Feb) to secure the best spots.
  • Check the tide times if you plan to walk around the headlands.
  • Consider hiring a buoy or securing valuables; beach thefts are rare but possible.
  • Respect any dog restrictions during summer months.
Local Provider

Equipment Hire & Lessons

Several local operators offer paddleboard and kayak hire, sometimes with guided tours. Look for notices on the beach or ask at the i-SITE for current providers.
