Golden Bay tastings

Wineries, cideries and tasting stops

Golden Bay is more boutique than big wine-region polished, which is part of the appeal. This page keeps the tasting ideas inside the site first, then lets visitors choose whether to click through to individual venues.

What to expect

Small-scale, local, and worth the detour

In this part of Golden Bay, the appeal is less about a polished wine trail and more about independent producers, house-made cider, and tasting rooms with real local character.

Hours can vary, especially outside summer, so it pays to check before setting off.

Quick tips
  • Plan tastings around a beach day or a Takaka stop rather than as a full-day wine circuit.
  • Some stops lean more distillery or cider than classic vineyard cellar door.
  • If you want the broadest list, the regional tasting guide is the best backup.
