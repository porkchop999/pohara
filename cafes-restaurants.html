Cafes and restaurants around Pohara

Start with easy local favourites in Pohara, then head into Takaka for more all-day cafes, bakeries, and casual spots. This page keeps the recommendations inside the Pohara site so visitors can browse without dropping straight into search results.

How to use this page

Good nearby options, without leaving the site first

Pohara itself is easy for a relaxed lunch, a drink after the beach, or dinner close to where you’re staying. Takaka adds more variety, especially for daytime coffee, cabinet food, and a broader cafe scene.

Opening hours can shift seasonally, so it is still worth checking each venue before you head out.

Quick tips
  • Pohara is best for easy beachside meals and sunset drinks.
  • Takaka is the better bet for daytime cafe hopping and coffee.
  • In summer and holiday periods, booking dinner ahead is smart.
